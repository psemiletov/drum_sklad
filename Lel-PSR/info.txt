LEL PSR (ЛЕЛЬ ПСР)

The legendary Soviet drum machine was develped at 1989 at SAM factory (завод Счетно-Аналитических Машин) by Sergey Shershnev (Сергей Шершнев). At the end of USSR, sound producers Tropillo and Vishnya recorded with LEL drum machine early albums of famous rock bands such as Kino, Strannye Igry, Aquarium (later, Kino used Yamaha RX-11).

LEL PSR has 12 samples
