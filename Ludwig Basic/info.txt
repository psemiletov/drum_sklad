Ludwig BASIC kit is based on free "Ludwig whole kit with Cymbals" by M-Rose Productions and composer-producer Gyom Amphoux (https://www.mroseproductions.com/freebies-1) with hihats additions from Scott McLean's Scott McLean_Ludwig Oyster Blue Pearl Drum Kit.

All original files were converted from 24-bit integer format to 32-bit float. All file names are original.

Gyom Amphoux's files are 48 kHz, Scott McLean's - 44.1 kHz

Hihats are Round-Robined, all other instruments are layered by the velocity.
