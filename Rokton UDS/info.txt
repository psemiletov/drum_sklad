Rokton UDS (Роктон УДС)

Rokton UDS was a successor of Formanta UDS, as far I know with the same specs. Not just a drum machine box, but a complete set of electronic drums with plates and pedals (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jq2ChEblJdQ).
Rokton UDS has two modes of timbres - presets i.e. samples (included with this Drumrox kit) and custom, where user can control the parameters of the sythesis.

Developers: Завод "Форманта", инженер Игорь Федосеев, дизайнер Сергей Наумов, пр-во КРЗ "Форманта", УПО "Вектор"
